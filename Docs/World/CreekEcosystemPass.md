# Creek Ecosystem Pass

Created: 2026-06-25

## Goal

Make creeks readable world structures: movement clue, survival pressure, sound cue, and biome transition.

## Layers

- channel edge;
- wet bank;
- riparian clutter;
- crossing candidate;
- water sound anchor;
- wet risk placeholder;
- gorge compression.

## Behavior

Creek-following can help, slow, redirect, or mislead. The creek should not become a guaranteed path.

## Evidence

Review creek head, wet bank, usable bank, crossing candidate, difficult crossing, and compressed creek corridor.

## Acceptance

A player can infer creek route logic from terrain, vegetation, sound, and footing cues without interface rescue.
