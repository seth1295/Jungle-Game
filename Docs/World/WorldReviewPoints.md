# World Review Points

Created: 2026-06-25

This is a developer-only checklist for future world screenshots and terrain review.

## Coast

Review beach, rock shore, creek mouth, cliff coast, headland, and natural dead-end coast movement.

## Creek

Review creek head, small channel, wet bank, crossing candidate, hard crossing, and waterfall placeholder.

## Ridge

Review ridge crest, spur shoulder, saddle, slope break, exposed slope, sheltered slope, and sky window.

## Lowland

Review basin floor, alluvial flat, wet lowland, regrowth gap, misleading clearing, and dense friction pocket.

## Gorge

Review gully entrance, compressed corridor, difficult exit, false shortcut, recoverable side slope, and water-cut obstacle.

## Evidence Later

Each point should eventually have terrain values, biome values, traversal class, first-person screenshot, and notes on what the player can infer from the world.
